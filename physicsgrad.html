General specifications for graduates of the Department of physics

  1. Good and basic knowledge of the structural and functional aspects of physical systems for multiple aspects, from a single part to an entire system.
  2. Connect basic ideas about the physical behavior of matter and energy to system construction and function
  3. Applying scientific knowledge and concepts to solving complex physical problems
  4. Design and conduct experiments and analyze and interpret data
  5. Work in multidisciplinary teams
  6. What are the professional and ethical responsibilities
  7. Communicate effectively
